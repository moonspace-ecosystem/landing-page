- **Annual Price Increases**: 5% annually for existing customers
- **Tier Migration**: 15% of customers upgrade tier annually
- **Enterprise Penetration**: Growing from 6% to 25% of customer base

#### Revenue Recognition
- **Subscription Revenue**: Monthly recognition for monthly plans, deferred for annual plans
- **Usage Revenue**: Recognized upon processing completion
- **Professional Services**: Recognized upon delivery completion

- **Annual Increases**: 10% salary increases, 15% for promotion/retention
- **Benefits Load**: 25% of salary (health, retirement, taxes)
- **Equity Compensation**: 10-15% of total comp for all employees

#### Infrastructure Scaling
- **AWS Costs**: Linear scaling with usage plus 20% efficiency gains annually
- **Third-party Tools**: Step-function increases at customer milestones
- **Security Investment**: 2% of revenue annually for SOC 2 and compliance

### Market Assumptions

- **Competitive Pressure**: Minimal for first 3 years, increasing thereafter
