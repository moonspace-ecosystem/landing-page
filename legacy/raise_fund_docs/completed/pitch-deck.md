# The Problem We're Solving

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8">
<div>

## 🎯 Core Challenge: Standards Fragmentation

**For Investors & Analysts:**
- 📊 **Inconsistent Standards**: Australian reports follow AASB, not IFRS/US GAAP
- ⏰ **Manual Conversion**: Hours of work to compare companies globally
- 🔍 **Complex Differences**: 200+ accounting standard variations
- ❌ **Error-Prone**: Manual processes lead to 15-30% error rates
- 💰 **Expensive Services**: Professional conversion costs $5K-50K per report

</div>
<div>

**For Companies:**
- 📈 **Limited Global Access**: Australian standards reduce international visibility
- 🏆 **Compliance Burden**: Dual reporting costs $100K+ annually
- 📋 **Investor Relations**: Difficult to attract international investment

## 💸 Market Pain Points
- Financial reporting software market: $2.8B
- 85% of international investors want IFRS/US GAAP format
- Average analyst spends 40% of time on data standardization

</div>
</div>

# Our Solution

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8">
<div>

## 🤖 AI-Powered Conversion Engine

**How It Works:**
1. **Upload** Australian AASB financial reports
2. **AI Processing** identifies and maps accounting standards
3. **Smart Conversion** to IFRS and US GAAP formats
4. **Validation** through ML-powered accuracy checks
5. **Export** standardized reports with audit trail

</div>
<div>

## 🌟 Key Innovation: Triple Validation
- ✅ **AI Pattern Recognition**: Identifies AASB-specific treatments
- 👥 **Expert Rules Engine**: 200+ conversion rules built by CPAs
- ⛓️ **Audit Trail**: Complete transformation documentation

## 💎 Value Propositions:
- **For Investors**: Instant global comparison capability
- **For Companies**: Affordable dual-standard reporting
- **For Analysts**: 90% time savings on data prep

# Product & Technology

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8">
<div>

## 🔧 Technical Architecture

**Frontend:**
- **Framework**: Qwik (ultra-fast performance)
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS with glassmorphism design
- **TypeScript**: Full type safety
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design

- **Runtime**: Bun.js for maximum performance
