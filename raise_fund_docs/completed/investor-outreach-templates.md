## 📊 Email Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Open Rate** | 25%+ | Subject line effectiveness |
| **Response Rate** | 15%+ | Email content relevance |
| **Meeting Conversion** | 50%+ | Of positive responses |
| **Time to Response** | <3 days | Investor interest level |

### A/B Testing Elements

**Subject Lines**:
- A: "Pre-Seed Opportunity: AI Platform for Financial Reporting"
- B: "$2.8B Market Opportunity: Australian Fintech Startup"

**Email Length**:
- A: Short version (150 words)
- B: Detailed version (250 words)

**Call to Action**:
- A: "15-minute call"
- B: "Quick demo"

### Response Analysis

**Positive Signals**:
- Requests for more information
- Questions about technical details
- Introduction to other team members
- Timeline discussions

**Neutral Signals**:
- Acknowledgment without commitment
- Requests to "stay in touch"
- Referrals to other investors

**Negative Signals**:
- Form rejection emails
- No response after follow-ups
- "Too early stage" feedback

## 🛡️ Email Best Practices

### Do's
- ✅ Personalize each email with specific investor details
- ✅ Keep initial emails under 200 words
- ✅ Include clear, specific call to action
- ✅ Follow up consistently but respectfully
- ✅ Track all interactions in CRM system

### Don'ts
- ❌ Send mass emails without personalization
- ❌ Overwhelm with too much information initially
- ❌ Follow up more than 3 times
- ❌ Use aggressive or pushy language
- ❌ Forget to include contact information

### Email Schedule
- **Initial Email**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM
- **First Follow-up**: 7 days after initial
- **Second Follow-up**: 7 days after first follow-up
- **Final Follow-up**: 14 days after second follow-up
